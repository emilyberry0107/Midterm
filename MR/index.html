The Maze Runner

IMDB Rate: 6.8/10

My Rate: 8.5/10

Synopsis

Thomas is deposited in a community of boys after his memory is erased, soon learning they're all trapped in a maze that will require him to join forces with fellow "runners" for a shot at escape (IMDB).
Review
The Maze Runner is the first film and book of its series. The film follows the book to a T and does not stray from what was written in the book. From how the female character was described in the book, I did not expect the actress that was chosen for the film. I feel that the actress did not protray the character very well, so I was not a big fan of the actor choice for this character. Aside from the choice of actress I feel that the film was excellently made, and the next two films made are even better.
